The GRCA is an international membership network of youth-centered arts and social justice organizations. This is a perfect place for those interested in the arts who want the freedom to explore themselves and their passions.




Girls Who Code is an international organization that urges for more women in STEM fields by educating them in coding. Whether girls take part in the Summer Immersion Program held by known monopolies or the Clubs, they are able to create bonds and a sisterhood with their fellow classmates. Partakers are inspired by the various stories taken from women of all ages and times. This program shows girls that taking risks is more important than trying to be perfect.
